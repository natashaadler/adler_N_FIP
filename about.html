mikito | natasha adler

Mikito derives from the Spanish word monkey. Much like the monkey species, Mikito promises a lifetime of flourishing, in design, leadership and the social environment. Mikito’s Key beliefs are positivity, impact and fun. Whether it’s the colours, the font or the logo, Mikito is meant to instil the essence of fun positivity and impact in any material produced. Anything produced by Mikito has a purpose. Mikito’s key pillars are positivity, responsibility, creativity and adjustability. Positivity is a crucial pillar, one must present each day with the best attitude to receive the best results. Responsibility, one must take ownership and care when approaching each task in an honest way, creativity is the expression needed for uniqueness and fulfillment of a project and adjustability, one must be able to adjust to the ever-changing environment and tasks.
